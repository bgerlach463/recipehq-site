Recipe Import

Add recipes any way you want

RecipeHQ accepts recipes however you find them — no reformatting required. AI handles the extraction.

  • Take a photo with your phone camera and AI extracts the recipe
  • Paste a screenshot or drop in any URL — we read the page for you
  • Upload a PDF cookbook and extract multiple recipes at once
  • Import your entire Google Keep recipe collection in minutes
  • Type recipes in manually with our built-in editor
  • Crop and frame photos before saving
  • AI detects multi-component meals and offers to split into separate recipes
  • Duplicate detection prevents importing the same recipe twice
  • Share any recipe with a link — no account needed for the recipient

AI Suggestions

Two ways AI tells you exactly what to cook

RecipeHQ's AI doesn't just store your recipes — it actively recommends what to cook based on two powerful signals: what you already have, and what you're already planning.

  • Cook from your fridge — tells you which recipes you can make right now using ingredients already in your pantry, prioritizing things that are about to expire
  • Plan smarter — once you have a few meals scheduled, suggests additions from your library that share the most ingredients with your current week
  • Both modes only draw from your own recipe library — no algorithm pushing random results at you
  • Suggestions update live as your pantry and plan change throughout the week
