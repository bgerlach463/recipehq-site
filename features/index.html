Recipe Import

Add recipes any way you want

MyRecipeHQ accepts recipes however you find them — no reformatting required. AI handles the extraction.

  • Take a photo with your phone camera and AI extracts the recipe
  • Paste a screenshot or drop in any URL — we read the page for you
  • Type recipes in manually with our built-in editor
  • Upload a PDF cookbook and extract multiple recipes at once
  • Import your entire Google Keep recipe collection in minutes
  • Crop and frame photos before saving
  • Editable tags — cuisine, protein, meal type — on every recipe
  • AI detects multi-component meals and offers to split into separate recipes
  • Duplicate detection prevents importing the same recipe twice
  • Share any recipe with a link — no account needed for the recipient

Ingredient Overlap

Plan meals that share ingredients

The core of MyRecipeHQ. See at a glance which meals stretch a single grocery run further — and which combos reduce waste the most.

  • Ingredient overlap counts show exactly how many items are shared across your week
  • "Plan My Week" auto-fills your entire week maximizing shared ingredients
  • Pick a recipe and get instant suggestions for what pairs best from your library
  • Multiple courses per meal — entree, side, dessert/snack in one slot
  • Anti-repetition — won't suggest the same meals you had recently
  • Savings summary shows shared ingredients across the week

Pantry Tracking

Know what's in your kitchen before you shop

Log what you buy, track when it expires, and get alerts before good food goes bad.

  • Unlimited items across fridge, freezer, and pantry sections
  • Expiration date alerts — days in advance, not the day of
  • Fresh / Expiring / Expired status at a glance
  • Suggest recipes that use what's about to expire
  • Tap "Mark as Cooked" on a planned meal and your pantry updates automatically
  • Snap a photo of your fridge — AI identifies items, tap how full each is, pantry stocked in seconds

AI Suggestions

Two ways AI tells you exactly what to cook

MyRecipeHQ's AI doesn't just store your recipes — it actively recommends what to cook based on two powerful signals: what you already have, and what you're already planning.

  • See which recipes you can make right now using ingredients already in your kitchen, prioritizing things about to expire
  • Once you have a few meals scheduled, get suggestions that share the most ingredients with your current week
  • Build a meal on the fly — suggest sides to complement any main dish
  • AI picks side pairings based on flavor, texture, and cuisine cohesion — not just shared ingredients
  • Smart Swaps — AI ingredient substitutions when you're missing something
  • Both modes only draw from your own recipe library — no algorithm pushing random results at you
  • Suggestions update live as your pantry and plan change throughout the week

Meal Planning

Your week on one screen

Plan 3 to 7 days at a time to match your shopping rhythm. Premium lets you set seed meals and AI fills the rest with overlap optimization. Max auto-plans your full week from scratch.

  • Plan 3-7 days — match your grocery run cadence
  • Set seed meals, AI fills the rest with overlap optimization
  • Full week auto-plan from scratch — AI pairs entrees with cohesive sides and skips sides for complete meals like pot pies
  • Meal view — dedicated cooking screen for the recipe you're making

  • Calorie and macro estimates on every recipe
  • AI-generated recipe images
  • Dinner-focused by default — no empty breakfast/lunch clutter
  • Add breakfast or lunch for any day with one tap
  • Navigate forward and back by week

Shopping List

From plan to store in one click

Generate your shopping list directly from planned meals. Items are AI-categorized by store layout so you can shop aisle by aisle, not recipe by recipe.

  • One-click generation from all planned meals
  • AI-categorized by store layout — produce, dairy, meat, frozen, pantry staples
  • Smart purchase quantities — recipe calls for 4 cloves garlic? Your list says 1 bulb
  • Flags items you may already have in your pantry
  • Check off items at the store
  • Move purchased items to your pantry with automatic expiration tracking
  • Snap a photo of your whiteboard or handwritten list — AI reads it and adds items
  • Add items manually for anything extra you need
