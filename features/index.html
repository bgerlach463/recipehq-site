AI Suggestions

Two ways AI tells you exactly what to cook

RecipeHQ's AI doesn't just store your recipes — it actively recommends what to cook based on two powerful signals: what you already have, and what you're already planning.

  • Cook from your fridge — tells you which recipes you can make right now using ingredients already in your pantry, prioritizing things that are about to expire Premium
  • Plan smarter — once you have a few meals scheduled, suggests additions from your library that share the most ingredients with your current week Premium
  • Build a Meal — suggest sides on the fly to complement any main dish Max
